## Full description (both stores, <=4000 chars)

You are not the victim. You are the corporation.

Prix & MiniThings is an idle clicker comedy about building a wholesale theft empire, one
trusting old man at a time. Tap to acquire your first collectible from Old-Timer Stan. Then
hire a workforce of Prix to automate the acquisition, stack synergies and multipliers, and
watch the counter climb while you do nothing at all.

When the heat arrives, Skip Town. Bank your Hush Money, rebrand, and reopen next quarter with
no prior litigation. That is not a bug. That is the business model.

FEATURES
- Idle and incremental: tap, automate, prestige, repeat.
- Dozens of upgrades, staff tiers, and stacking multipliers.
- Bespoke minigames: crack the vault, spin the wheel, survive the raid.
- Offline earnings - the franchise robs while you sleep.
- A deep prestige layer (Skip Town and Hush Money) that resets the run and keeps the meta climbing.
- Built to run fully offline. One purchase. No ads. No in-app purchases.

Prix & MiniThings is satire. Every character, company, church, and senator is invented. No
actual old people were harmed in the making of this franchise. Probably.
