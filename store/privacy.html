Privacy Policy

Prix & MiniThings . Operated by P&M Corporate . Last updated 30 June 2026
The short version. Prix & MiniThings is a paid game with no ads, no third party trackers, and no analytics SDKs. You can play the whole game without an account. If you choose to sign in, we store only what is needed to sync your progress. We never sell your data.

Who we are

This app is operated by P&M Corporate ("we", "us"). For any privacy question, contact josh@pamcorporate.com.

What we collect, and why

Most of the game runs on your device. We only collect data when a feature needs it:

  • Email address - only if you choose to sign in. We use a one time code (no password). It identifies your account so your save can sync and referrals can be credited.
  • Game progress - your save state (cash, upgrades, prestige), so you can continue across devices when signed in.
  • Referral codes - if you use or share one, so the referral bonus can be applied.
  • Bug reports - only when you tap "Found a bug?". This sends a diagnostic session log so we can fix issues. It contains gameplay events, not personal content.
  • Technical data - our backend provider may process standard request data such as IP address and timestamps to deliver and secure the service.

If you never sign in, your progress stays on your device in local storage and is not sent to us.

What we do not do

  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No third party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No selling or renting of personal data.
  • No profiling or cross app tracking. We do not ask to track you across other apps or websites.

Third parties

  • Supabase - our backend for sign in, database, and save sync. Data is stored on their infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play - distribute the app and process your purchase. Your payment is handled by the store, not by us. We never see your card details.

Children

Prix & MiniThings is a satirical game intended for a teen and older audience. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Keeping and deleting your data

We keep account and save data while your account exists. To delete your account and associated data, email josh@pamcorporate.com from your account email and we will remove it. You can also clear local data at any time from the in game settings (Wipe).

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these, contact us at the email above. We do not sell personal information.

Security

Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS). Access to stored data is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Changes

If we change this policy we will update the date above and, for material changes, note it in the app.
